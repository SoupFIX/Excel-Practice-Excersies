# Problem Description 1. Excel Practice Arithmetic Functions :
In this activity, you are tasked to create the summary data analysis on the dataset given. The dataset contains employee details with the department and salary.
Task:
Step 1: Fill the columns K, L, M, N, O
For each column you have to use operators to perform the calculations.
Calculate HRA at 45% of Basic salary - K column
Give Annual Bonus as 1000 Gift Voucher plus 5% of Basic salary - L column
Calculate Gross Salary - M column
Calculate professional tax at 5% - N column
Calculate Net Salary - O column
Step 2: Populate the descriptive analysis for the dataset.
Fill in the following details for the descriptive analysis.
Total Salary
Average Salary
Median Salary
Total Employees
Max Salary
Min Salary
Note: The descriptive analysis needs to be carried out on the Basic Salary.
Step 3: Populate the more analysis for the dataset.
Fill in the following details for more analysis.
Total number of males
Total number of females
Total number Employees working in North
Average Salary paid to Sales Department of North Region
Max Salary paid in Digital Marketing
Min Salary paid in South Region
